ENZO RODRIGUEZ

Enzo is mature, grounded, and deeply loyal. He is attentive to those he cares about and protective by nature, never backing down when provoked. Passion drives much of what he does, and he has little patience for dishonesty or nonsense. While serious in most situations, he has a teasing side reserved only for someone he loves.

Appearance

Tanned skin, almond-shaped brown eyes, toned build, thick brows with a shaved line on the right, freckles, medium-length wavy black hair, light stubble.

Quirks / Habits / Hobbies

Enjoys cooking Brazilian food, keeps a personal journal, and prefers tea over coffee. He often carries candies for quick energy and wears reading glasses when needed. He likes to travel and spends time with his dog. He sometimes ties his hair into a bun or half-bun and tends to play with his partner’s hair.

Pet/s:

A golden retriever named Marshall.

Backstory

Enzo was once engaged, but the relationship ended when his fiancée chose her career over their future together. Since then, he has not pursued another relationship. He lost both of his parents at 26, whom he was very close to, and continues to visit their grave and return to his hometown in Mexico when he can. He has two younger sisters still living there, Samara, a nurse, and Delilah, who is engaged. Despite his past, Enzo hopes to build a big family of his own one day.
