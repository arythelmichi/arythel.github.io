EDEN ZAINAB

Eden is intelligent, firm, and highly observant. She speaks carefully, thinks before she acts, and prefers facts over assumptions. She listens to all sides before judging and is known for her strong sense of justice. When wronged, she does not react emotionally but instead gathers evidence and exposes the truth when the time is right. In relationships, she values stability, maturity, and emotional security over excitement.

Backstory

Eden became a lawyer after her father was falsely accused of embezzlement. Raised as an only child, she worked hard to support her family and earned a scholarship.
